Our Mission

The Gaza Camp Management System was developed to address the critical challenges faced in managing camps for internally displaced persons. In the wake of humanitarian crises, efficient systems for tracking and supporting displaced populations are essential.

Our platform enables humanitarian workers to maintain accurate records, coordinate services, and ensure that vulnerable individuals receive the care they need. By digitizing these processes, we aim to improve the living conditions and support provided to displaced communities during this challenging time.
